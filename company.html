Tomas Arguinzones

Tomas has over 19 years of IT experience and over 12 years of Jira and the whole Atlassian ecosystem experience. He has worked on Atlassian application related projects in both large and mid-size companies such as Priceline.com, CocaCola, DirecTV, AT&T, Aviat, Corelis, MobileWash and several dozens more.
